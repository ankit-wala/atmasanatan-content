---

> **How to read this index**
> - Entries are ordered by **first occurrence in the calendar window**, grouped by Hindu month.
> - Each row shows: order number ¬∑ slug (folder name) ¬∑ festival name ¬∑ **exact tithi** ¬∑
>   approximate English date ¬∑ book-selection marker.
> - Dates marked ‚úì are verified against DrikPanchang. Dates marked `~` are derived from
>   tithi anchors ‚Äî verify against DrikPanchang and update `date_2026`/`date_2027` in
>   `meta.yaml` before setting `status: reviewed`.
> - **VS** = Vikram Samvat. VS 2083 ‚âà Apr 2026 ‚Äì Apr 2027.
>   VS 2084 ‚âà Apr 2027 ‚Äì Apr 2028. The VS year transitions on Chaitra Shukla Pratipada.
> - Hindu months span across English months ‚Äî this index uses the *English date* as the
>   sort key but **the tithi (Hindu month + paksha + day number) is the canonical identity**
>   of every festival. Two festivals can share an English date but have different tithis.
> - `[108]` = selected for the flagship 108-katha book (order 1‚Äì108 in `meta.yaml`).
>   `[R]` = reserve pool (order 200+) ‚Äî available for mini-books, future editions, or
>   replacing any [108] entry that gets dropped during editing.
> - **Total corpus: 150 entries.** The book is curated from this pool ‚Äî we do not write
>   exclusively for the 108; we write everything and select.
